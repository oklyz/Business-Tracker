Entrepreneurs:

This website will provide entrepreneurs with tools and insights to help them determine whether their business is on the right track. It will also open opportunities to connect with potential investors and access loan facilities from banks. In addition, the platform will guide entrepreneurs in recognizing when adjustments are needed or even when it may be better to pivot or close the business so they can avoid wasting time and resources.

Investors:

Investors will gain transparent access to the business's balance sheet and income statement, supported by clear profitability charts and insights. This makes it easy to evaluate growth potential and return on investment. For those interested in moving forward, they can directly contact the business owner via phone or email, ensuring a smooth path to partnership.

Line Managers:

This platform connects business owners with banks, making it easier for owners to request loans when needed. It will also provide line managers with financial insights based on the income statement and balance sheet that highlight whether the business represents a safe or risky lending opportunity.
